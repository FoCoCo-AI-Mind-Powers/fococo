## SECTION 2 — Mind & Game Links
- **Title:** Mind & Game Links
- **Subtitle:** See what truly drives performance.

### Layout
- Horizontal scroll of **5 insight tiles**.
- Each tile = one AI-detected correlation between **Mind (mental data)** and **Game (performance metrics)**.

### Insight Tile (structure)
- **Headline:** short AI-generated link summary  
  - Example: When Focus above 80, Approach Accuracy +9%.
- **Key Stat Line:** correlation statement  
  - Example: When Confidence MPI is 75+, Fairways Hit is +8%.
- **Body (2 sentences):** where/when/why context  
  - Example: Based on your last 3 rounds in windy conditions at Monte Rei. Confidence routines remained most consistent.
- **Tags (pills):** e.g. Focus / Visualization / Windy
- **Buttons:**
  - View on FoCoMap (visualize where pattern appears)
  - Open LogBook Round (related round details)
  - Start Visualization Booster (launch recommended MindCoach session)

**Microcopy (bottom):** Each tile reveals how your mind and game interact. The more you log, the clearer these patterns become.

## SECTION 4 — Cues & Routines Effectiveness
- **Title:** Cues & Routines Effectiveness
- **Subtitle:** Find what truly works for you, and when.

### Sub-sections
1) **Top MindCue**
- Shows usage count + impact vs baseline
- Example: Self-Talk used 12 times. Increased overall MPI +6 pts vs baseline.

2) **Timing Impact**
- Compare: Pre-Shot vs Between Shots vs Post-Shot
- Bar cards comparing impact on next-shot outcome
- Example: Pre-Shot routines reduced errors on Par-3 tee shots by 9%.

3) **Reset Moments**
- Trigger success rate of recovery within a 2 hole stretch
- Example: Reset routine after a bogey equates to +62% chance of making par on the next hole.

### Buttons
- Add to Training Plan → adds MindCue or Routine to MindCoach Session
- See moments on FoCoMap → highlights when/where used

**Microcopy (bottom):** The more you log your Routines and MindCues, the smarter your personal analytics become.
