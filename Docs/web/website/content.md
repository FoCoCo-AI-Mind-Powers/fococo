FAQ
Q1. What exactly is FoCoCo? 
FoCoCo is The AI Golf Mind Coach. A performance system that connects your thoughts, 
reactions, and decisions to the results you see on the course. 
Q2. How does FoCoCo help my golf? 
FoCoCo improves clarity, decision making, momentum management, and emotional 
steadiness. The mental factors that influence consistency and scoring. 
Q3. What makes FoCoCo different from other golf apps? 
Swing apps track mechanics. Data apps track stats. FoCoCo tracks you, your clarity, 
reactions, resets, momentum, and decision patterns. 
Q4. Do I need to talk to my phone while I play? 
No. Talking is completely optional. 
You can log everything through taps or typed notes at any time. 
JustTalk is there if you want fast, hands-free logging, but FoCoCo works perfectly without 
voice. 
Q5. Does FoCoCo talk back to me during the round? 
Only if you want it to. FoCoCo offers built in voice guidance, visual prompts, or silent text 
responses. 
Q6. Will FoCoCo lower my scores? 
FoCoCo improves the mental factors that influence scoring, with clearer decisions, 
steadier reactions, stronger focus, and faster recovery after mistakes. 
When these areas improve, golfers often see more consistency in their rounds and 
naturally move toward lower scores through better choices. 
FoCoCo doesn’t change your swing, it strengthens the mind behind every shot. 
Q7. Is FoCoCo good for beginners? 
Yes. Beginners gain structure, confidence, and clarity without anything technical or 
complicated. 
Q8. Is FoCoCo useful for competitive or experienced golfers? 
Yes. FoCoCo helps experienced golfers understand patterns, protect momentum, stay 
composed under pressure, and make more consistent decisions at higher levels of play. 
Q9. Can I use FoCoCo for practice sessions, not just rounds? 
Yes. You can log routines, reflections, and mindset notes during practice. 
Q10. How does JustTalk work? 
JustTalk activates only when you turn it on. 
Speak naturally and FoCoCo records the moment. 
If you prefer silence, you can switch to taps or typed notes instantly, the system adapts in 
real time. 
Q11. What if I don’t want to use voice at all? 
All voice features are optional and can be turned off globally. 
Q12. Does FoCoCo replace a swing coach? 
No. FoCoCo complements technical coaching by strengthening the mental and decision￾making side of performance. 
Q13. Can FoCoCo be used on the course in real time? 
Yes. FoCoCo supports on course, real time logging. Including voice, taps, typed notes, and 
optional GPS based context. 
Q14. Does FoCoCo use GPS or location data? 
Yes. FoCoCo can use GPS for shot location, lie context, course conditions, and round 
mapping, but GPS is optional and can be turned off anytime. 
Q15. What is FoCoMap? 
FoCoMap blends your mindset patterns, decisions, tendencies, shot outcomes, and 
performance into three clear visual maps of your round. 
Q16. What is the Mind Power Index (MPI)? 
MPI shows your strengths across Focus, Confidence, and Control. How those mental 
patterns influence momentum and scoring trends. 
Q17. What devices are supported? 
FoCoCo is available on iOS, Android, and the FoCoCo Web App. 
Q18. Can I use FoCoCo without internet? 
Yes. You can log rounds or moments offline. Your insights sync when you’re back online. 
Q19. Is FoCoCo allowed during tournaments? 
Rules differ between clubs and events, especially regarding electronic assistance during 
play. Many golfers use FoCoCo before or after rounds. Always check with the event 
organizer. 
Q20. Is FoCoCo a medical, psychological, or therapeutic tool? 
No. FoCoCo does not diagnose, treat, or manage medical or psychological conditions. It is 
not therapy or counseling. 
FoCoCo is a golf performance and decision making system focused on clarity, routines, 
reactions, and the mental skills used in sport. It helps golfers understand patterns and 
make better choices, nothing more. 
If you have concerns about mental or emotional wellbeing, please seek support from a 
licensed professional.
