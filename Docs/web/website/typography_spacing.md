************************************************************************************************* 
GLOBAL TYPOGRAPHY RULES 
1. Desktop 
• Hero Headline (H1 | Hero) 
• 48px - 600 (Semi-Bold) 
• Line-height: 1.15 
• Letter-spacing: –0.02em 
• Section Headline (H2 | Section Titles) 
• 36px - 600 (Semi-Bold) 
• Line-height: 1.2 
• Letter-spacing: –0.01em 
• Subtitle (H3 | Sub-Section Titles) 
• 22px - 500 (Medium) 
• Line-height: 1.35 
• Body Text (Paragraphs) 
• 16px - 400 (Regular) 
• Line-height: 1.60 
• Micro-Title Labels (Feature Labels / Mini Headlines) 
• Example: 
• (MindMap, ShotMap, SyncMap, Log Your Game, etc.) 
• Size: 18px 
• Weight: 600 (Semi-Bold) 
• Line-height: 1.35 
• Spacing above: 24px 
• Spacing below: 8–12px 
• Always left-aligned 
2. Mobile 
• Hero Headline (H1 | Hero) 
• 34px - 600 (Semi-Bold) 
• Line-height: 1.20 
• Section Headline (H2 | Section Titles) 
• 26px - 600 (Semi-Bold) 
• Line-height: 1.25 
• Subtitle (H3 | Sub-Section Titles) 
• 18px - 500 (Medium) 
• Line-height: 1.35 
• Body Text (Paragraphs) 
• 15px - 400 (Regular) 
• Line-height: 1.60 
• Bullets / Supporting Items 
• Same as Body 
• Weight: 400 (Regular) 
• Line-height: 1.55–1.60
